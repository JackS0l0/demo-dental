Dental Care Services

Dental Implants

A missing tooth can lead to serious health issues. The roots of a tooth stimulate the tissue in your jawbone, keeping it strong. When you lose a tooth, the lack of root structure causes your jawbone to deteriorate, leading to problems like further tooth loss and sunken facial features. Titanium or ceramic dental implants act as artificial tooth roots, effectively stopping further bone loss.

Learn More About Dental Implants

Invisalign

Many patients would like a straighter smile but hesitate due to the thought of wearing metal braces. Invisalign® is the perfect solution for these patients. The discreet clear plastic aligner trays of the Invisalign system allow for greater comfort and no dental hygiene or dietary restrictions. There are limitations to candidacy, but for those who qualify, Invisalign offers a nearly invisible treatment option.
